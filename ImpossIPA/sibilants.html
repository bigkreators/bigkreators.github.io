Sibilant fricatives:

  • Bilabial ɸⓈ βⓈ
  • Labiodental fⓈ vⓈ
  • Linguolabial s̼ z̼
  • Dental s̪ z̪
  • Alveolar s z
  • Postalveolar s̠ z̠
  • Retroflex ʂ ʐ
  • Palatoalveolar ʃ ʒ
  • Alveolo-palatal ɕ ʑ
  • Palatal çⓈ ʝⓈ (or potentially ɕ̠ ʑ̠)
  • Post-palatal ç̠Ⓢ ʝ̠Ⓢ (or potentially ɕ̠̠ ʑ̠̠)
  • Velar xⓈ ɣⓈ
  • Post-velar x̠Ⓢ ɣ̠Ⓢ
  • Uvular χⓈ ʁⓈ
  • (Upper-)pharyngeal ħⓈ ʕⓈ
  • Epiglottal ʜⓈ ʢⓈ
  • Glottal hⓈ ɦⓈ

Then for the affricates just pre-stop them:

  • Bilabial pɸⓈ bβⓈ
  • Labiodental p̪fⓈ b̪vⓈ
  • Linguolabial t̼s̼ d̼z̼
  • Dental ts̪ dz̪
  • Alveolar ts dz
  • Postalveolar ts̠ dz̠
  • Retroflex ʈʂ ɖʐ
  • Palatoalveolar tʃ dʒ (or maybe perhaps ȶ̟ʃ ȡ̟ʒ)
  • Alveolo-palatal ȶɕ ȡʑ
  • Palatal cçⓈ ɟʝⓈ (or potentially cɕ̠ ɟʑ̠) Post-palatal cç˗Ⓢ ɟʝ˗Ⓢ (or potentially cɕ̠˗ ɟʑ̠˗)
  • Velar kxⓈ ɡɣⓈ
  • Post-velar kx˗Ⓢ ɡɣ˗Ⓢ
  • Uvular qχⓈ ɢʁⓈ
  • (Upper-)pharyngeal ꞯħⓈ 𝼂ʕⓈ
  • Epiglottal ʡʜⓈ ʡʢⓈ
  • Glottal ʔhⓈ ʔɦⓈ
